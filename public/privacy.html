Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

What Mandime Is

Mandime is a personal application operated by a single individual for their own use. It is not a public service, commercial product, or platform available to other users. It exists solely to help its operator manage and publish their own video content to their own social media accounts.

Data Collected

Mandime does not collect, store, or process data from any third party or end user. The only data accessed is:

  • The operator's own TikTok account information, accessed via TikTok's official Content Posting API with the operator's own authorization.
  • Content (videos, captions, metadata) that the operator creates and chooses to publish.

No data from other TikTok users, visitors, or any third parties is collected, stored, or processed.

Authentication and Credentials

TikTok OAuth tokens and API credentials are stored locally on the operator's own machine. They are used solely to authenticate with TikTok's API and publish content on the operator's behalf. Credentials are never transmitted to any third party.

No Tracking, Profiling, or Sharing

Mandime does not:

  • Track or profile any users.
  • Use cookies, analytics, or tracking pixels.
  • Share, sell, or disclose any data to advertisers, data brokers, or third parties.
  • Use data for any purpose other than publishing the operator's own content.

TikTok's Data Handling

When content is published to TikTok via their API, TikTok's own data practices apply. Review TikTok's privacy policy at tiktok.com/legal/page/us/privacy-policy.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated if the application's functionality changes. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any revisions.
