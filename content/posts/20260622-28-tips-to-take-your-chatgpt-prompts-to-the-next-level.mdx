If you thought ChatGPT was just about asking for the weather, think again. David Nield breaks down 28 tips to level up your prompts and get way more out of the bot. From specifying the format to adding context, these tricks can turn a mundane query into something genuinely useful. For instance, asking for a summary in bullet points or requesting a response in a specific tone can yield much better results. The key is to be as clear and detailed as possible. So, next time you hit up ChatGPT, remember: the more you put in, the more you get out.
