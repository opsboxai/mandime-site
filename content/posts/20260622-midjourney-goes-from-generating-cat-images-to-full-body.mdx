Midjourney CEO David Holz just unveiled a full-body ultrasound scanner, a massive leap from the company’s usual cat pictures. Dubbed The Midjourney Scanner, this device uses a ring of sensors to capture vertical images, turning AI-generated visuals into a medical marvel. Holz also announced plans to open a San Francisco spa, a move that's as unexpected as it is ambitious. The scanner could revolutionize how we approach full-body imaging, making it faster and more accessible. But with this shift, Midjourney is stepping into a highly regulated field, where accuracy and safety are paramount. It's a bold move, and if it works, it could set a new standard in medical imaging.
