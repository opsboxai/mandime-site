Harvard astrophysicist Avi Loeb, the guy who keeps insisting we've found alien tech, is now heading the UAP Science Advisory Council. The White House, Pentagon, and other top agencies are backing this council to "resolve the nature of UAP," or unidentified anomalous phenomena. 

Loeb's credentials are solid, and his team includes a diverse group of experts from various fields. But his reputation has taken a hit due to his persistent claims about alien life. Since 2015, he's been making headlines with theories like Oumuamua being an alien probe and small metal spheres in the ocean being alien wreckage. 

While Loeb's Harvard pedigree and long career have kept him in the spotlight, many in the scientific community see his claims as more sensational than scientific. So, it's a wild choice to have him lead this council. But hey, if anyone can turn UFO research into a media circus, it's Avi Loeb.
