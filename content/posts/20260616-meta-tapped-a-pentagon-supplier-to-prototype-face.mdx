Meta teamed up with Rank One Computing, a Pentagon supplier, to develop face recognition tech for their smart glasses. Rank One, known for its ties to former CIA and FBI officials, provided the tech for Meta's internal app development. This partnership raises questions about privacy and the integration of advanced surveillance capabilities into consumer tech. Dell Cameron and Dhruv Mehrotra break down the implications of this collaboration, highlighting the intersection of government tech and everyday devices.
