catching big halibut usually means hitting the open waters, but Fisherman's Life shows you can do it from shore. in this video, they break down how to target these massive fish using a few key techniques. first, they recommend finding the right spot—areas with deep drop-offs and rocky structures where halibut tend to hang out. using heavy-duty gear is crucial, as halibut can easily break lighter lines. they also suggest using cut bait or whole fish as bait, as halibut are attracted to the scent and movement. the real trick is patience; it can take hours to get a bite, but the fight is worth it. if you're looking to land a trophy without leaving the beach, this guide is a must-watch.
