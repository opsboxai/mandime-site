OpenAI just dropped a new version of GPT-5.5-Cyber, aiming to patch open-source software bugs. Amid growing concerns about AI models and cybersecurity, this move is a direct shot at Anthropic’s Mythos. The “Patch the Planet” initiative is OpenAI’s full-scale effort to address vulnerabilities in open-source software. It’s a smart play to show they’re not just about building powerful AI but also about securing it. This isn’t just about tech; it’s about proving they can be a responsible player in the AI game.
