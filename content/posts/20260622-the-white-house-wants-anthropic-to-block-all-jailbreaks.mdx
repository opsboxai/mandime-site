The White House is pushing Anthropic to block all jailbreaks in their AI model, Fable 5. But security experts are skeptical it can be done. The request comes from Trump administration officials who want to ensure the model's guardrails can't be circumvented. The reality, however, is that creating an impenetrable system might be an impossible feat. As these experts point out, if there's a way in, there's a way around it. This tension between government demands and technical realities highlights the ongoing struggle to control AI's capabilities.
