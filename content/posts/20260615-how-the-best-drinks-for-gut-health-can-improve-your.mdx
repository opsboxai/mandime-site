If you're looking to boost your gut health, you might want to start with what you drink. David Taylor breaks down some of the best drinks that can improve your digestion and overall health. Probiotics and prebiotics are key here, and they can be found in drinks like kombucha, kefir, and even certain types of tea. These beverages not only add beneficial bacteria to your gut but also provide the food for those bacteria to thrive. So, next time you're at the grocery store, consider picking up a bottle of kombucha or a can of kefir. Your gut will thank you.
