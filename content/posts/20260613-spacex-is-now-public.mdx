SpaceX just went public, and it's a big deal. The rocket company, led by Elon Musk, started trading on Nasdaq on June 12th at $135 per share. While that's the official price, most retail investors will likely end up paying much more. This IPO is historic, and Andrew J. Hawkins breaks down the details in this piece. SpaceX's journey from a private venture to a publicly traded company is a significant moment in the tech and space industries.
