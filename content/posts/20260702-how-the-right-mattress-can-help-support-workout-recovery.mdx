Sleep is when your body rebuilds stronger, and if you're hitting the gym hard, you need every advantage to recover. According to Dr. Nneoma Oparaji, sleep is when your body shifts into repair and recovery mode, crucial for processes like tissue repair and growth hormone release. But if you're not getting quality sleep, your gains could be at risk. Research shows that athletes who regularly sleep less than eight hours a night see their injury rates almost double, and their strength and speed suffer.

That's where your mattress comes in. A good mattress can make a huge difference in your recovery.
