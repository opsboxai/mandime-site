Driving over potholes might seem like a minor inconvenience, but it can seriously mess with your ride. Supercar Blondie breaks it down in her latest video, showing just how much damage those little craters can do to your car. From bent rims to shattered glass, it's a crash course in why you should steer clear.

Potholes aren't just unsightly; they're a real threat to your vehicle's health. A single hit can lead to misaligned wheels, damaged suspension, and even blown tires. And if you're unlucky enough to catch one at speed, you might end up with a cracked windshield or broken headlights. It's not just about the repair costs, driving with compromised components can affect your car's handling and safety.

Supercar Blondie's video is a stark reminder of the importance of vigilant driving, especially in areas known for potholes. The footage shows the immediate aftermath of hitting a few, and it's not pretty. Bent rims and shattered glass are just the beginning. The real kicker is the potential for hidden damage that could cost you big in the long run.

So, next time you're cruising down a bumpy road, take a moment to scan for those pesky potholes. It might save you a lot of headache, and money, down the line.
