NOMOS Tangente Neomatik 38 Update: This watch is a game-changer, not just for its stunning green dial but for its Bauhaus design that's been perfected over three decades. The forest green dial, a first for NOMOS, is a deep, muted evergreen that looks almost black in low light. The ring date complication, which took home a GPHG award, is a standout feature, with two red markers framing the current date, creating a striking color contrast. The 38.5mm case is sleek and thin, making it a perfect desk watch. The black Horween Shell Cordovan strap is a premium touch, and the in-house DUW 6101 automatic movement is a marvel of finishing at this price point.

Bellroy Apex Slim: Bellroy's Everglade colorway is a standout, with a muted, gray-washed sage that looks almost like eucalyptus. The Apex Slim wallet is not just about looks; it's die-cut, pre-molded, and heat bonded, resulting in clean edges with zero thread. The magnetic closure is satisfying, and the wallet holds about six to eight cards and a few folded bills. The LWG gold-rated leather is soft and already broken in, making it a perfect addition to any EDC setup.

Leatherman Micra: The Micra has been a staple for years, and the new Cerakote treatment with a two-tone Spruce colorway makes it a perfect fit for this green EDC loadout. The two handle scales sport different shades of green, adding a unique touch. The 420 stainless steel frame and ceramic coating ensure durability and a sleek look. Whether you're a minimalist or just looking to add a pop of color to your EDC, these pieces are must-haves.
