The Busy Bar, a productivity multitool from the minds behind the Flipper Zero, is finally hitting the market on July 14th. And it's not just a simple desk gadget; it's a status display that can help you stay focused and communicate your availability to others. For the first 3,000 units, you can snag one for $199, but it's normally priced at $249. 

The Busy Bar is essentially an alarm clock with a twist. It's designed to sit on your desk, atop your monitor, or even mounted on a wall. The 72 x 16 LED pixelated screen displays a countdown timer or status message, letting everyone know when you're in focus mode and when you'll be available again. It's like a digital "do not disturb" sign that actually works.

What's really cool is its integration with other devices. The Busy Bar can mute notifications, start a Pomodoro timer, and even trigger smart home automations like dimming lights or playing music. And with an open API, developers can create third-party apps to expand its functionality. Imagine syncing it with your office scheduling system to indicate when meeting rooms are booked. 

If you're looking to boost your productivity and reduce distractions, the Busy Bar might just be the tool you need. Just hit the button, and let it do the talking.
