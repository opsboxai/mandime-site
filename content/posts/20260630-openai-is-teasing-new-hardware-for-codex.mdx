OpenAI and Work Louder are teaming up to release a new device on July 15th, and it's got the tech world buzzing. The teaser video shows a square-shaped gadget with several buttons, promising to upgrade your favorite Codex shortcuts. While it's not the mysterious AI device OpenAI has been working on with Jony Ive, this collaboration with Work Louder is still a big deal.

Work Louder is known for its mechanical keyboards and macro pads, like the Creator Micro 2, which comes with 13 mechanical switches, a joystick, and a touch sensor. Users can map shortcuts and custom actions to these toggles, making it a game-changer for coders and designers. The Codex-focused device seems to be following a similar path, offering a hands-on way to streamline your coding process.

With just two weeks to go, we're all eagerly waiting to see what this collaboration brings. Will it be a macro pad with Codex shortcuts preconfigured? A new type of keyboard with AI-powered features? Whatever it is, it's clear that OpenAI and Work Louder are aiming to make coding more efficient and accessible. Stay tuned.
