Sketchy Andy Lewis, the legendary pioneer of slacklining and BASE jumping, tragically died in a Moab accident. Fred Dreier reported that Andy, known for pushing the absolute limits of human flight, met his end while BASE jumping. His daring feats and innovative spirit have left an indelible mark on the outdoor adventure community. Andy's death is a significant loss for those who admired his fearless approach to extreme sports.
