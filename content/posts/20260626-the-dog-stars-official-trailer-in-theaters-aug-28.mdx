Fear the survivors. Ridley Scott's latest, "The Dog Stars," is a post-apocalyptic thriller that drops in theaters on August 28. Starring Jacob Elordi, Josh Brolin, and Margaret Qualley, this film promises to be a gritty and intense ride.

Based on Peter Heller’s bestseller, "The Dog Stars" is a tale of survival in a world ravaged by a pandemic. The trailer shows a bleak landscape where the few survivors are as dangerous as the virus itself. Josh Brolin, always a force to be reckoned with, plays Hig, a man struggling to maintain his humanity in a world stripped of it. Jacob Elordi, known for his roles in "Euphoria" and "The Kite Runner," takes on the role of George, Hig's young and volatile sidekick. Margaret Qualley, with her striking presence, rounds out the main cast as a mysterious figure in this desolate world.

The ensemble also includes Allison Janney, Benedict Wong, and Guy Pearce, each bringing their own gravitas to the film. With a screenplay by Mark L. Smith and production by Ridley Scott, "The Dog Stars" is set to be a visual and emotional spectacle. Scott's track record with intense, visually stunning films like "Blade Runner 2049" and "The Martian" suggests that "The Dog Stars" will not disappoint.

If you're into post-apocalyptic thrillers and are looking for a film that delves deep into survival and human resilience, "The Dog Stars" is one to watch. The trailer alone is enough to pique the interest of any fan of the genre. Fear the survivors, indeed.
