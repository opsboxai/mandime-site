California's new tire regulations might just be the bane of every car enthusiast's existence. The proposed program aims to restrict the types of tires available, which could mean a serious hit to the performance and customization options for car lovers. While the move is intended to improve safety and reduce emissions, it could also drive up the cost of tire replacement for everyone. This isn't just about saving a buck; it's about preserving the freedom to tweak and tune your ride the way you want. If you're into pushing your car's limits, keep an eye on this one.
