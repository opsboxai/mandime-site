Chris Bumstead shows off a killer leg stretch that'll have you touching your toes in no time. The former bodybuilding champ breaks down a simple yet effective routine to boost leg flexibility. He starts with a basic lunge, then transitions into a deep stretch that targets the hamstrings and quads. The key is to hold each position for at least 30 seconds, breathing deeply to ease into the stretch. This isn't just about touching your toes; it's about improving your overall mobility and preventing those nagging injuries. So, if you're looking to add some serious flexibility to your workout routine, give this a try.
