Diesel engines are built to last, and it all comes down to one word: compression. Michael Frank breaks it down in this piece from Jalopnik. Diesel engines operate at much higher compression ratios than gas engines, which means they can extract more energy from the fuel. This efficiency and the robust construction of diesel engines contribute to their longevity. So, if you're looking for a vehicle that can handle the miles, a diesel might just be your best bet.
