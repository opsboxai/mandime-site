Chris Bumstead, aka "Lionheart," just dropped a video that'll make you rethink your gym gear. In it, he's stripping down his setup to the essentials, and it's a damn eye-opener. The dude who's known for his no-nonsense approach to lifting is showing us what really matters in the gym. 

He starts with the obvious: weights, barbells, and dumbbells. But then he gets into the nitty-gritty, ditching things like foam rollers and resistance bands. "You don't need all that crap to get strong," he says, and it's hard to argue with a guy who's won multiple strongman titles. 

The takeaway? Sometimes less is more. If you're cluttering your gym bag with gadgets and gizmos, maybe it's time to hit the reset button. Focus on the basics, and you might find your progress skyrockets. 

Lionheart's video is a reminder that strength and progress come from consistency and hard work, not from having the latest gear. So next time you hit the gym, leave the unnecessary stuff at home.
