The 2026 World Cup is set to be a massive global event, but not everyone will get to join the party. Many Arab fans might face visa issues, making it nearly impossible for them to attend. Malak Saleh highlights the barriers and borders that could keep these fans locked out. The promise of a global celebration is marred by the harsh reality of visa restrictions, which can turn what should be a unifying event into a frustrating and exclusive one. For fans who have saved up and planned for years, the bureaucratic hurdles could be a significant letdown. This isn't just about missing a soccer match; it's about the broader issues of access and inclusion in global events. The World Cup should be a moment of unity, but visa policies might end up dividing fans instead of bringing them together.
