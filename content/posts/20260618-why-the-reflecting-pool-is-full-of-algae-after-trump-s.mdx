The Reflecting Pool at the National Mall is now a murky green mess, thanks to a recent algae bloom. Warm weather has accelerated the growth, and National Park Service workers are scrambling to clean it up before the July 4 celebrations. They're using everything from hydrogen peroxide to nanobubbles to combat the algae. It's a stark reminder that even after renovations, nature can still throw a wrench into the works.
