Over 15 years after The Social Network hit theaters, we finally get a peek at The Social Reckoning. This time, Jeremy Strong steps into the role of Mark Zuckerberg, taking over from Jesse Eisenberg. The trailer, which dropped ahead of the film's October 9th release, shows a more mature Zuckerberg navigating the tech giant's legal and ethical challenges. The Social Reckoning promises to delve deeper into the inner workings of Facebook, with a young engineer challenging the company's practices. Sorkin's script and Strong's performance are sure to keep us on the edge of our seats.
