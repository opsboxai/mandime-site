José Andrés knows a thing or two about teamwork and survival, both in the kitchen and beyond. At Outside Days, the celebrated chef and humanitarian dropped some serious wisdom on why people who love the outdoors are the best of humanity. Andrés emphasized the importance of coming together, not just in the face of adversity but also in celebrating the beauty of nature. His insights aren't just for chefs; they’re for anyone looking to make a positive impact. Whether you’re scaling a mountain or running a restaurant, Andrés’ lessons on resilience and community are worth a listen.
