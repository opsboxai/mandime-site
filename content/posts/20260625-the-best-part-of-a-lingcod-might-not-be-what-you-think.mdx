If you thought the best part of a lingcod was its meat, think again. Addicted Fishing breaks down why the real star of the show might be something else entirely. In this video, they dive deep into the unique qualities of lingcod, revealing that its liver is a culinary gem. 

The liver, often overlooked, is rich in flavor and texture, making it a prized delicacy among seafood enthusiasts. Addicted Fishing shows how to properly prepare and cook the liver, highlighting its buttery, almost decadent taste. They also share some tips on how to source lingcod and why it's worth the effort to try this lesser-known part of the fish.

For guys who love to fish and cook, this video is a must-watch. It's not just about the meat anymore; it's about discovering new flavors and elevating your fishing game. So next time you catch a lingcod, don't just filet it, save that liver and give it a try. Trust us, your taste buds will thank you.
