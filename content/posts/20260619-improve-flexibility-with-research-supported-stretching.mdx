Flexibility isn't just about touching your toes. In this Huberman Lab Essentials video, Andrew Huberman breaks down the science behind stretching, showing why it's crucial for injury prevention and performance. He dives into research-supported protocols that can help you improve your flexibility without wasting time on ineffective methods.

Huberman explains that dynamic stretching, which involves moving parts of your body and gradually increasing reach, range of motion, and speed of movement over time, is key for enhancing flexibility. Static stretching, where you hold a stretch in a challenging but comfortable position, can be beneficial but should be done at the right time, like post-workout, to avoid reducing muscle strength.

The video also highlights the importance of consistency. Just like any other fitness goal, flexibility gains come from regular practice. Huberman suggests integrating these stretching protocols into your routine a few times a week to see real improvements.

For guys looking to stay active and avoid injuries, this video is a must-watch. It's not just about feeling limber; it's about optimizing your body's performance and longevity.
