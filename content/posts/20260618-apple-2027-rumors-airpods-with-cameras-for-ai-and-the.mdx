Apple's latest rumor mill is churning out some wild stuff. Mark Gurman is reporting that the tech giant is on track to launch camera-equipped AirPods in late 2027. These aren't just any cameras; they're designed to power AI features, making your earbuds more than just noise-cancelling gadgets. 

But that's not all. Gurman also hints at a second folding iPhone, adding another twist to the already crowded foldable phone market. The idea of having a camera in your earbuds might sound like something out of a sci-fi movie, but if Apple pulls it off, it could redefine how we interact with our devices. 

The camera-equipped AirPods could potentially track your head movements, enhance augmented reality experiences, or even monitor your health. Meanwhile, the folding iPhone could offer a larger screen in a more compact form, catering to those who want a bigger display without the bulk.

While these are just rumors for now, they give us a glimpse into where Apple might be headed. If you're into tech and always looking for the next big thing, these rumors are worth keeping an eye on.
