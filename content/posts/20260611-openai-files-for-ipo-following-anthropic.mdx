OpenAI just took a big step towards going public. On Monday, the company confidentially filed a Form S-1 with the US Securities and Exchange Commission, putting them in the running alongside rival Anthropic, which made a similar move back in June. This marks a significant milestone in the race to IPO, with both companies vying for a piece of the lucrative AI market. Hayden Field breaks down the implications of this move, showing how OpenAI is positioning itself for a major shift in its corporate structure.
