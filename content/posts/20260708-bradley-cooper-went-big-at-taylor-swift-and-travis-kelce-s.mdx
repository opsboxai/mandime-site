Bradley Cooper showed up to Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce's wedding with a watch that could double as a compass. The Hollywood actor was spotted wearing an IWC Big Pilot’s watch, which clocks in at a whopping 46mm. That's a full 10mm bigger than most watches on the market today. 

Cooper's choice of timepiece is a bold move, especially at a wedding where a dress watch might seem more appropriate. But let's face it, when you're at a star-studded event with Paul McCartney singing, you need to stand out. The Big Pilot’s watch isn’t just about looking good; it's also practical. With a seven-day power reserve, it's perfect for those long, no-phone weddings where you need to keep track of the time without checking your phone.

It's clear that Cooper isn't just about following trends. He's got a sense of style that's all his own. Whether he chose the watch for its size, its practicality, or just to hold his own among the NFL players in attendance, it's a move that's hard to ignore. So, if you're looking to make a statement at your next big event, maybe it's time to break out the biggest watch in your collection.
