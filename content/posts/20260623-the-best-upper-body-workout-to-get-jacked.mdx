If you're looking to pack on some serious upper-body muscle, you don't need a gym full of equipment. Dean Stattmann breaks it down to just four exercises that will rip your top half to shreds. 

Start with pull-ups, the ultimate test of upper-body strength. They work your back, biceps, and shoulders, hitting multiple muscle groups at once. Next, hit the bench press to build those chest muscles and add some serious mass. 

Don't forget about the military press, which targets your shoulders and triceps, giving you that full, rounded look. And for some extra burn, throw in some dips. They hit your triceps, chest, and shoulders, ensuring you're working every part of your upper body.

These exercises aren't just about lifting weights; they're about building a strong, defined physique. So, skip the endless bicep curls and focus on these four moves to get jacked.
