buc-ee's in texas is next level. one location holds not one, but two world records. the store in lewisville is the world's largest convenience store, covering over 86,000 square feet. meanwhile, the san antonio location boasts the world's longest convenience store, stretching over 300 feet. these aren't your average gas stations. everything is bigger in texas, and buc-ee's proves it. thanks to adrian taylor at jalopnik for the eye-opening details.
