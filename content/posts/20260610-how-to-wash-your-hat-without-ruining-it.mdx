Because there’s nothing worse than getting forehead acne from a sweat-stained ball cap. Coleman Molnar breaks down the simple steps to keep your hat clean without ruining it. First, turn it inside out and use a gentle detergent. Gently scrub the inside with a soft brush, then rinse thoroughly. Let it air dry away from direct sunlight. This guide ensures your favorite hat stays fresh and comfy, saving you from those pesky breakouts.
