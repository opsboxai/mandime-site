Hoonigan tackled the Bronco's biggest issue head-on. The Ford Bronco is a beast, but its off-road capabilities are often overshadowed by one glaring flaw: its mediocre on-road comfort. Hoonigan put this one through the ringer, addressing the Bronco's bumpy ride with a comprehensive suspension upgrade.

They started with a set of Fox Performance 2.0 Internal Bypass shocks, which offer better control and smoother handling. Adding to the setup, Hoonigan installed a leveling kit to maintain the Bronco's stance while improving ground clearance. The result? A Bronco that can handle both the highway and the trail with equal aplomb.

For guys who want a truck that can do it all, this upgrade is a no-brainer. It's not just about off-roading; it's about making the Bronco a daily driver that doesn't compromise on comfort. Hoonigan's video is a must-watch for Bronco owners looking to elevate their ride without losing its rugged charm.
