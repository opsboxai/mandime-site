the owyhee river in eastern oregon is a rugged beauty. writer owen clarke recently joined a packrafting trip down this remote waterway during its dry season. the lower owyhee is known for its stunning landscapes and challenging access, making it a perfect playground for those who love a good adventure. packrafting, with its lightweight and portable boats, offers a unique way to explore these hard-to-reach areas. clarke's journey highlights how packrafting can turn low water levels into an advantage, allowing adventurers to navigate spots that would be off-limits to larger boats. it's a reminder that sometimes, the best trips are the ones that require a bit of ingenuity and a willingness to embrace the unexpected.
