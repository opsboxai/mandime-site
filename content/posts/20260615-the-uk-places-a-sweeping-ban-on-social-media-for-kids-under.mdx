The UK is cracking down hard on social media for kids under 16. The government plans to ban these platforms from targeting children, aiming to protect them from harmful content. Joel Khalili and Ben Gartside report that this move also sets a minimum age for some chatbots, adding another layer of safety. It's a big step to keep young people away from the darker sides of the internet.
