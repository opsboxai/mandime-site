The Trump administration is easing up on Anthropic’s AI models, Mythos and Fable, after months of tension. The Commerce Department is now lifting export controls, meaning these advanced AI systems can be shared more freely. This move comes after Anthropic agreed to implement stricter safeguards to prevent users from bypassing safety restrictions.

The deal marks a significant shift in Anthropic’s approach. Initially, the company argued that the administration's concerns were overblown, insisting that it was nearly impossible to eliminate all potential security risks. However, Anthropic has since changed its stance, agreeing to work closely with the government to address these concerns. This includes proactively detecting and addressing security risks and building more robust safeguards to reduce the number of jailbreaks.

The decision to lift export controls is a win for Anthropic, but it also highlights the ongoing tension between technological advancement and national security. As AI continues to evolve, the balance between innovation and safety remains a critical issue.
