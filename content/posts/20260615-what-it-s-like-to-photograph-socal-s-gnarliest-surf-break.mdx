Dingo, a 60-year-old surf photographer, recently shot a massive summer swell at Newport Beach’s most famous wave, The Wedge. The gnarliest surf break in SoCal is no joke, and Dingo has been capturing its power for decades. His latest photos show the riders shredding through towering waves, a true test of skill and courage. If you’re into waves and photography, these shots are a must-see.
