Scientists have finally pinpointed the source of those mysterious repeating radio signals from space, and it's a big deal. These fast radio bursts (FRBs) have puzzled astronomers for years, but now researchers say this discovery could be a “Rosetta stone” for understanding cosmic signals. The source, located in a nearby galaxy, could unlock secrets about the universe's most energetic events. This isn't just a win for space nerds; it's a major breakthrough that could rewrite our understanding of the cosmos.
