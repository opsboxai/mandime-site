Jeff Nippard breaks down the age-old debate: does the negative or positive phase of a lift matter more for gains? In this video, he dives deep into the science and practical applications, showing why both phases are crucial but for different reasons. The negative, or eccentric, phase helps with muscle damage and growth, while the positive, or concentric, phase is key for strength and power. Jeff's analysis is thorough, making it clear that focusing on both can lead to better overall results. So, next time you hit the weights, remember to give both phases their due.
