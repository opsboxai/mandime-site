Move over, beef tallow. Sardines are the new skincare sensation taking over TikTok. Yes, you read that right, those little oily fish are now being touted as the ultimate skin savior. But before you dive into a can, let's break down what's actually happening here.

Sardines are packed with omega-3 fatty acids, which are known for their anti-inflammatory properties and ability to help retain moisture in the skin. According to Lisa Moskovitz, RD, CEO of The NY Nutrition Group, omega-3s can help reduce inflammation and support a healthy skin barrier. This means that sardines might help calm down mild acne and eczema, and keep your skin hydrated and resilient.

But let's be clear: sardines aren't a miracle cure. While they can contribute to better skin health, genetics, hormones, stress, and sleep all play significant roles too. Eating sardines regularly could make your skin look a bit clearer and more hydrated, especially if you're not already getting enough omega-3s in your diet. However, they won't erase wrinkles, tighten loose skin, or create an overnight glow.

So, if you're already a fan of sardines, keep enjoying them. They fit nicely into a Mediterranean-style eating pattern, which is linked to better overall health. Just remember, sardines are one piece of the puzzle. Maintain a balanced diet, stick to your skincare routine, and don't forget to apply SPF daily. Sardines can be a helpful addition, but they're not a replacement for the essentials.
