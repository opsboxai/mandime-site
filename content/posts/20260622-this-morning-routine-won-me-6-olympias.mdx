Chris Bumstead, a six-time Olympia winner, breaks down his morning routine in this video. He starts with a cold shower, not just to wake up but to boost his mental and physical toughness. "It's not about the water," Bumstead says, "it's about how you handle the discomfort." 

He follows the cold shower with a strict protein shake, emphasizing the importance of hitting his macros right from the start of the day. "I need to fuel my body for the day ahead," he explains. The routine also includes a meticulous stretching and mobility session, which he credits for his longevity in the sport.

Bumstead's approach isn't just about physical preparation; it's about mental fortitude. "Consistency is key," he notes, "and this routine keeps me focused and disciplined." His insights offer a glimpse into the dedication and commitment required to stay at the top of bodybuilding.
