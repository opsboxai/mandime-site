The 2026 Subaru Forester is a prime example of how far automotive safety has come. With its Advanced EyeSight driver assist system, it's not just about surviving a crash; it's about preventing one altogether. The Forester's safety features are top-notch, with a "Good" rating from the IIHS in every major category. It's a testament to how Subaru has transformed the Forester into a modern, safe SUV.

But the Forester isn't alone. The 2026 Mazda CX-30 and Honda Passport also earn "Top Safety Pick+" ratings from the IIHS. The CX-30, with its budget-friendly Porsche-like driving dynamics, offers a comprehensive array of driver-assist features, making it a standout in its class. Meanwhile, the Honda Passport, with its rugged off-road focus, comes packed with advanced safety tech like the Honda Sensing suite, ensuring you're protected whether you're on the highway or hitting the trails.

These SUVs aren't just about safety; they're about peace of mind. Whether you're a family looking for a reliable ride or an adventure-seeker hitting the roads, these vehicles offer the latest in safety technology to keep you and your passengers secure. So, the next time you're in the market for an SUV, remember: safety isn't just a feature; it's a priority.
