Bentley's first all-electric crossover, the Torcal, is set to make waves in the luxury EV market. The teaser image reveals a sleek, modern design with a crystal-like, quilted pattern in the taillights, giving it a distinctive look that's both futuristic and unmistakably Bentley. At over 16 feet long, this beast promises a range of more than 300 miles, putting it in league with some of the best in the game.

The Torcal's design is a blend of Bentley's classic aesthetic and cutting-edge technology. The front-end is more squared-off and sharper than its gas-guzzling sibling, the Bentayga, with hard angles and sharp daytime-running lights that echo the Flying Spur and Mulsanne sedans. Inside, the attention to detail is top-notch, with ASMR-worthy materials like woods, metals, fabrics, and leathers that make it a true luxury ride.

Named after El Torcal de Antequera in Andalusia, Spain, the Torcal is more than just a car; it's a statement. Bentley promises that every decision, element, and characteristic of this EV is centered around the customer. With its September 23 reveal looming, the Torcal is shaping up to be a game-changer in the luxury EV segment. Stay tuned for more details as the launch date approaches.
