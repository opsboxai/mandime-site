Turns out, hip thrusts aren't just for the girls. Chris Bumstead breaks down why this move should be in every guy's routine. Hip thrusts aren't just about building a bigger, stronger butt; they're about improving your overall lower body strength and stability. Bumstead shows how this exercise can help you lift more, run faster, and even prevent injuries. The key is in the technique—keep your core tight, drive through your heels, and control the descent.
